About Us

Our Mission

At Sanjeevini Cancer Seva Trust, we are devoted to providing compassionate care and essential support for cancer patients.
We strive to offer both practical assistance and emotional comfort, ensuring that those in need receive the help and resources necessary during this challenging time.

Our Vision

Provide Financial Assistance in Cancer Diagnosis & Treatment
Conduct Educational Workshops
Increase Community Outreach to Create Awareness about cancer.
SANJEEVINI

Objectives of the Sanjeevini Cancer Seva Trust

Financial assistance to the poor & needy patients who have been diagnosed mainly with cancer or with any other ailments, and to assist in their diagnosis, investigations, & treatment
To establish, promote, set up, run, maintain, assist, support, and/or aid to or help in the setting up, starting, and maintaining and running cancer institutes, hospitals, medical colleges, paramedical colleges & other institutions in India. To promote advance treatment and care facilities for the benefit of patients irrespective of their caste, creed, gender, and religion.
Transportation assistance to the patients & their caretakers to the centre of treatment
Accommodation of the patients & caretakers till the completion of the treatment
Follow up of the patients treated with the assistance of the organization
Social and psychological support/counseling to the families of the patients who are being treated
Creating social awareness about the cancer disease and importance of early treatment.
To set up an old age home for the needy in a self sustainable model by collecting moderate fees.
